World-Class Export Solutions

We manage every step of your export journey — from farm procurement to custom clearance at the destination port.

🔬
Quality Assurance & Testing
Rigorous lab testing, moisture analysis, and visual inspection at every stage of production and packing.
  • FSSAI & international food safety compliance
  • Third-party lab testing available
  • Certificate of Analysis with every shipment
📦
Custom Packaging & Branding
OEM packaging solutions — your brand, your label, export-grade packaging that meets international standards.
  • Vacuum, kraft, and food-grade packaging
  • White-label / private label options
  • Custom sizes: 100g to 25kg bags
  • Eco-friendly packaging available
🚢
Freight & Logistics
Flexible shipping modes — sea, air, and multimodal — with real-time tracking and end-to-end coordination.
  • FCL & LCL container options
  • Air freight for urgent shipments
  • Reefer containers for sensitive products
  • Door-to-port and door-to-door available
📋
Documentation & Compliance
Complete handling of export documentation — APEDA registration, phytosanitary certificates, invoices, and more.
  • Phytosanitary & fumigation certificates
  • Certificate of Origin (COO)
  • APEDA & DGFT compliance
  • Letter of Credit (LC) support

— Our Story

Built on Trust & Quality

Manjusha Enterprises was founded in 2010 in Pune, Maharashtra with a simple mission — to bring the finest Indian agricultural products to the world with integrity and transparency.

Over the past 15+ years, we've built deep relationships with farmers across Maharashtra, Rajasthan, Bihar, and other states — ensuring our products are always fresh, authentic, and of the highest calibre.

Today, Manjusha Enterprises proudly exports to 30+ countries, serving importers, distributors, health food brands, and retail chains around the world.

🎯
Our Mission
To connect India's agricultural wealth with the global market, delivering products that uphold the highest standards of quality, purity, and sustainability.
👁
Our Vision

💎
Our Values
